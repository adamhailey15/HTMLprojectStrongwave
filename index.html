Bio

An independent artist, rocking from Wakefield (near Leeds), in the UK. My goal with music production is
to createuplifting music which provides a unique and enjoyable experience for the listener, instigating
mind numbing chills through synth driven drops, and providing melodic hooks that sit still in your mind for days.

I produce a diverse selection of genres, ranging from Melodic Future Bass, to Hard hitting Trap and Dubstep.
I like to produce music which inspires me, and my hopes are that listening to it will inspire you too!
